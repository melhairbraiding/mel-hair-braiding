Who We Are

About Us

Our Story

Born from a love of natural hair and patient hands.

Mel Hair Braiding was founded in Charlotte with one simple belief: every client deserves a stylist who listens first, braids second. What started as weekend sessions in a home studio grew into a full-service protective styling space built on trust, technique, and genuine care.

"I learned to braid in my grandmother's kitchen. Every style I do carries that memory."

Today, Mel and her team serve clients across Charlotte with the same intention that started it all — clean parts, comfortable tension, and styles that protect your hair while keeping you looking polished.
