Before you arrive

A little prep keeps the install smooth.

Please arrive with hair washed, detangled, and stretched unless your appointment includes prep. Bring inspiration photos, note any scalp sensitivity, and plan extra time for waist-length or small-part styles.

01

Clean hair with no heavy oils or gels gives the neatest finish.

02

Sleep with satin and moisturize your scalp lightly between washes.

03

Book removal or touch-up before the style starts pulling or matting.
