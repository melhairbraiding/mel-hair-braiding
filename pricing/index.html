Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to bring my own hair?

You can bring your own braiding hair or purchase it through us at cost. Just let us know your preference when booking.

How do I prepare my hair?

Arrive with hair washed, detangled, and stretched. No heavy oils or gels. If you need prep services, add it on when booking.

How long will my style last?

Most styles last 4–8 weeks with proper care. We'll send aftercare instructions after your appointment.
