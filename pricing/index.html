Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to bring my own hair?

You can bring your own braiding hair or purchase it through us at cost. We carry a range of extension types and colors. Just let us know your preference when booking.

How do I prepare my hair for the appointment?

Please arrive with hair washed, detangled, and stretched. No heavy oils, gels, or buildup. If you need prep services, add it on when booking and we'll take care of it.

How long will my style last?

Most styles last 4–8 weeks with proper care. We'll send you aftercare instructions after your appointment to help you get the most out of your style.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice to cancel or reschedule without penalty. Late cancellations forfeit the $50 deposit. No-shows are charged the full deposit.
